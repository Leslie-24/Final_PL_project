# BI Requirements - Expired Goods Auto-Detection System

## KPIs That Matter
1. **Expiry Prevention Rate (EPR)** - % of near-expiry products sold before expiration
2. **Alert Response Time (ART)** - Avg hours to resolve expiry alerts  
3. **Discount Effectiveness Ratio (DER)** - Revenue recovered from discounted products
4. **Waste Reduction Value (WRV)** - Monthly reduction in expired product losses
5. **Staff Resolution Rate (SRR)** - % of alerts resolved by assigned staff
6. **System Uptime Percentage (SUP)** - Availability of auto-detection system

## Decision Support Needs
1. **Daily**: Which products need immediate attention today?
2. **Weekly**: Which categories have highest expiry risk?
3. **Monthly**: What discount strategy is most effective?
4. **Quarterly**: Which suppliers provide longest shelf-life?
5. **Strategic**: How to optimize inventory ordering?

## Stakeholders
| Role | Information Needs | Key Decisions |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| **Store Managers** | Real-time alerts, daily expiry list | Staff assignment, discount approval |
| **Inventory Supervisors** | Category expiry trends, stock turnover | Purchase orders, stock rotation |
| **Finance Department** | Discount impact, waste reduction | Budget allocation, pricing strategy |
| **Operations Team** | System performance, process efficiency | Workflow optimization, training needs |
| **Senior Management** | Overall system effectiveness, ROI | Strategic planning, investment decisions |
